CodeOp's Solo Project, Jan 2021
Description
Makanbah! is a restaurant recommendations website, which allows it's users to search for restaurants nearby them and also create a personalised bucket-list of their restaurant choices. The name "Makan bah!" was derived from the word "Makan", which means "Eat" in the English language, and "bah" from Sabahan slang which is a colloquial words used in Sabah, Malaysia. With Makanbah!, you don't have to worry again where to eat next!
